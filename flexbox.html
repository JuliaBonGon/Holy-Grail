ANIMALS HABITAT
  • Jungle
  • Sea
  • Farm

Animals thrive in a variety of habitats, each offering unique environments and resources essential for their survival.
The jungle provides dense foliage and diverse flora, offering shelter and camouflage for its inhabitants like monkeys, tigers, and exotic birds.
In the sea, an expansive and mysterious realm, marine creatures such as dolphins, sharks, and colorful fish navigate vast waters rich in nutrients and interconnected ecosystems.
On the farm, domesticated animals like cows, chickens, and horses benefit from human care, grazing on cultivated fields and enjoying sheltered spaces designed for their well-being. These three habitats showcase the adaptability and diversity of animal life across our planet.
